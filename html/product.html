Spec Section

Current Physical Specs (plans to reduce size and weight)

  • Dimensions 1 x 2 x 3 inches
  • Weight: approximately 70 grams

Video Modes

  • Resolution VGA or QVGA
  • Frame Rate 15 to 30 fps
  • HD streaming up to 720p. Video can be configured between to conserve power.
  • Video Format: H.264 Codec

Audio

  • Format 32Hz sampling rate
  • AGC (automatic gain control)
  • AGC (automatic gain control)

Current Battery Specs (plans to reduce power consumption)

  • Charge may last up to 6 days
  • 30 min of streaming a day equals 3days before recharging is required while in event mode or constant streaming of 2.5 hours
  • Rechargeable Lithium Ion Battery
  • Rated at 3.7 V 1450 maH
  • Charge port is a micro USB
